Research Topic: Future of Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

- AI is transforming healthcare by improving diagnostics, patient care, drug discovery, and hospital operations.
- Machine Learning models are achieving near-radiologist level accuracy for detecting diseases.
- NLP is helping predict diseases early through patient record analysis.
- Robotic surgeries are becoming more precise and minimally invasive.
- Healthcare AI market projected to reach $67 billion by 2027.
- Ethical challenges include bias, data privacy, and accountability of AI systems.
