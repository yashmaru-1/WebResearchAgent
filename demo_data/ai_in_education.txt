Research Topic: Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Education

- AI is revolutionizing personalized learning by adapting lessons to student progress.
- Intelligent Tutoring Systems provide real-time feedback and customized problem sets.
- AI is automating administrative tasks, saving time for teachers.
- Chatbots are supporting student queries outside classroom hours.
- AI-driven career guidance platforms suggest optimal paths based on aptitude.
- Ethical concerns include data privacy of students and algorithmic bias.
